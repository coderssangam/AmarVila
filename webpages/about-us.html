OUR STORY

Founded in 2005 by Mr. Amar Nath Arora, Hotel Amar Vila was established with a vision to provide exceptional hospitality and a home-like experience to every guest. Located in the heart of Indore, our hotel quickly became known for its prime location, dedicated service, and warm ambiance. Over the years, Hotel Amar Vila has earned a reputation for excellence, offering guests well-appointed rooms, a multi-cuisine restaurant, elegant banquet halls, and personalized service that ensures comfort and satisfaction. Whether you're here for business, leisure, or celebration, we are committed to making your stay memorable with the best in modern amenities and traditional hospitality. At Hotel Amar Vila, your comfort is our priority, and we look forward to welcoming you with the warmth and care that defines who we are
